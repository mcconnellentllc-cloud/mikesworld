Our Roots

A Legacy Rooted in Colorado Soil

The Peterson family's connection to Colorado agriculture stretches back to the early 1900s, when the first generation of Petersons put down roots in the fertile plains of northern Colorado. What began as a small homestead has grown into a thriving multi-generational farming operation that remains true to the values established over a century ago.

Today, Mike and Kim Peterson carry on this proud tradition as 3rd generation farmers, while raising the 4th generation to understand the value of hard work, respect for the land, and the importance of producing food the right way.

Through the Years

Our Family Timeline

Early 1900s

Roots Take Hold

The Peterson family first established their presence in northern Colorado, putting down roots in the agricultural heartland. The first generation began working the land, planting the seeds of a farming legacy that would span more than a century.

Mid-1900s

Second Generation

The second generation of Petersons took the reins, expanding the family's agricultural operations and deepening their commitment to responsible land stewardship. They passed down the values of hard work, integrity, and respect for the animals and land that sustain us.

1993

Mike & Kim Begin Their Journey

Mike and Kim Peterson began their own chapter in the family farming story. Armed with generations of knowledge and a deep love for the land, they started building what would become Pasture Perfect Premium Beef, continuing the family tradition on their farm in Sedgwick, Colorado.

2000s

Growing the Herd

The Petersons expanded their Black Angus cattle operation, focusing on quality over quantity. They committed to raising their cattle on open pastures with farm-grown feed, developing the practices that would define their premium product. Their children began learning the family trade.

2010s

Regenerative Practices

Embracing regenerative agriculture, the Petersons refined their approach to land management. Growing their own feed reduced their carbon footprint and ensured complete control over the quality of their product. The 21-day dry-aging process was perfected.

2020

Pasture Perfect Premium Beef

The Petersons officially launched Pasture Perfect Premium Beef, bringing their decades of experience and premium product directly to consumers. Registered with the Colorado Secretary of State, the brand represents over a century of agricultural knowledge.

Today

4th Generation & Beyond

Now as 3rd and 4th generation farmers, the Peterson family continues to raise their children with the same appreciation and respect for agriculture. They partner with local businesses like River Bear American Meats and Hook & Hock, sharing their premium beef with more Colorado families than ever.

Our Values

What the Petersons Stand For

Family

Family First

Relationships with our family, animals, and customers always come first. We know how much hard work it takes to run a farm and raise a good family at the same time.

Land

Respect for the Land

We practice regenerative agriculture, growing our own feed on our farm to reduce our carbon footprint and ensure the land thrives for generations to come.

Care

Animal Welfare

Our cattle are raised the way they deserve — basking in open pastures with the care and respect that leads to both happy animals and exceptional beef.

Roots

Community

We are a small family operation that is part of a small rural community. Supporting local business and Colorado agriculture is at the heart of everything we do.

Quality

Legacy

Passing Down Knowledge

Today & Tomorrow
