Mike & Kim
Meet the Petersons

Mike & Kim Peterson

Mike and Kim Peterson know how much hard work it takes to run a farm and raise a good family at the same time. Their family has been farming for generations — you could say farming is in their blood.

With strong ties to Colorado agriculture since the early 1900s, we are 3rd and 4th generation farmers and ranchers raising our family with the upbringing and education we know is essential to making homegrown food.

We are a small family operation that is part of a small rural community, raising our kids to appreciate and respect what agriculture in the US has been for centuries. As you get to know us, you'll find that relationships with our family, animals, and customers always come first.

Our Approach

How We Raise Our Beef

I

Open Pastures

Our Black Angus cattle bask in open Colorado pastures, living as nature intended before receiving a carefully managed diet.

II

Farm-Grown Feed

We grow our own feed right here on our farm. This allows us to reduce our carbon footprint and ensure quality from seed to steak.

III

Regenerative Farming

We manage our pastures using regenerative agriculture approaches to ensure sustainability for generations to come.

IV

Grain Finished

A grain-finished diet adds to the richness of flavor and marbling. While regenerative farming is our method, exceptional flavor is our promise.

Proud Colorado Partners

We're proud to partner with local businesses who share our commitment to quality. Our partnership with Hook & Hock and River Bear American Meats has helped bring Pasture Perfect Premium Beef to even more Colorado families.

When you buy from our Colorado family farm, you're supporting local business and Colorado agriculture. We believe in building relationships — with our land, our animals, and our customers.
