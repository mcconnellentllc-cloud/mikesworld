Our Story

Farming is in Our Blood

Mike and Kim Peterson know how much hard work it takes to run a farm and raise a good family at the same time. Their family has been farming for generations — you could say farming is in their blood.

With strong ties to Colorado agriculture since the early 1900s, we are 3rd and 4th generation farmers and ranchers raising our family with the upbringing and education we know is essential to making homegrown food.

Why Choose Us

Our Promise to You

Regenerative farming is our method. Exceptional flavor is our promise.

I

Pasture Raised

Our cattle bask in open pastures, enjoying the Colorado sunshine before receiving a grain-finished diet that adds to the richness of flavor.

II

Farm-Grown Feed

We feed our animals with the crops we grow on our own farm, reducing our carbon footprint and ensuring the highest quality nutrition.

III

USDA Certified

All our beef is processed at USDA certified facilities, and each cut is meticulously inspected to meet the highest standards.

IV

21-Day Dry Aged

Our beef is masterfully dry-aged for 21 days, intensifying its flavor and tenderness for an unforgettable eating experience.
